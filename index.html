PROJECTS:

Shakespeare Word Analyzer

Built using the C++ Qt toolkit , the Shakespeare Word Analyzer provides an intuitive desktop GUI for users to make queries and find specific words used by William Shakespeare. Users can search in two manners, they can find words based on their word length and frequency, or they can opt for the search bar, where they simply type out the word and search for it.

Hollywood Social Network

Have you every wondered if two actors have intersecting social networks? By providing the first name and last name of two actors, Hollywood Social Network will use a graph data structure to find their "degrees of separation" and will return all adjacent actors between them using IMDb data.

Pathways Website

Each year, many companies, organizations, and foundations provide Black and Latinx students with prestigious scholarships, internships, and apprenticeships. A lot of the time, it’s hard to find these opportunities since there isn’t a central hub for all of these resources. Inspired by this, our goal was to build an-all-in one platform where students can find scholarships, full time roles, and internships geared toward innovative Black and Latinx undergraduates.
